About RentalHub

Our Mission

RentalHub is dedicated to making the process of finding and managing rental properties simple, transparent, and secure. We connect tenants with quality rental homes and provide landlords with powerful tools to manage their properties efficiently.

What We Offer

For Tenants

  • Wide selection of verified rental properties
  • Advanced search and filtering options
  • Easy booking and payment process
  • Maintenance request management
  • Transparent rental agreements

For Landlords

  • Property listing and management tools
  • Tenant screening and approval system
  • Payment tracking and history
  • Maintenance request handling
  • Analytics and reporting

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book a property?

A: Browse available properties, select one you like, and click "Request to Rent". The landlord will review your request and respond within 48 hours.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers (demonstration only for this school project).

Q: How do I report maintenance issues?

A: Log in to your tenant dashboard and navigate to the maintenance request section. Fill out the form describing the issue and submit it to your landlord.

Q: Can I cancel a booking?

A: Cancellation policies vary by property. Contact the landlord directly or check your booking details for specific terms.

Q: How do I become a landlord?

A: Register an account, select "Landlord" as your user type, and once approved, you can start adding properties.
